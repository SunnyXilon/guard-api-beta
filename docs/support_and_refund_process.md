# Support And Refund Process

## Support Channel

Create a dedicated support inbox before launch:

```text
support@your-domain.example
```

Every support request should include:

- Workspace name.
- Customer email.
- Request ID, if available.
- Approximate time of the issue.
- Expected behavior and actual behavior.

## Support Targets

- Trial users: response within 2 business days.
- Starter: response within 1 business day.
- Growth: response within 8 business hours.
- Scale: response within 4 business hours.

## Refund Rules

Approve refunds for:

- Duplicate charges.
- Accidental upgrade reported within 7 days.
- Major service outage that prevents normal use.
- Billing mistake caused by plan or webhook configuration.

Usually do not refund:

- Completed months of normal usage.
- Quota exhaustion from legitimate traffic.
- Accounts suspended for acceptable-use violations.

## Refund Workflow

1. Confirm the Stripe customer and subscription.
2. Check usage and incident history.
3. Decide full refund, partial refund, credit, or denial.
4. Record the reason in support notes.
5. Process the refund in Stripe.
6. Reply with the result and expected bank timeline.
