# Refund Policy Draft

This draft is a starting point and should be reviewed before public launch.

## Free Trial

New customers may receive a limited free trial if advertised on the pricing page. Trial credit quota and duration are subject to plan terms.

## Monthly Subscriptions

Customers can cancel future renewals from the billing portal or by contacting support. Cancellation does not automatically refund the current billing period unless required by law or explicitly approved.

## Refund Requests

Refunds may be considered when:

- A duplicate payment was made.
- The service was unavailable for an extended period.
- The customer was charged after a confirmed cancellation.

Refunds are generally not provided for consumed API usage, abuse-related suspension, or failure to integrate the API correctly.

## Abuse

Accounts suspended for abuse, fraud, policy violations, or chargeback misuse may be ineligible for refunds.
