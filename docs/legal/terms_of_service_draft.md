# Terms of Service Draft

This draft is a starting point and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before public launch.

## Service

Guard API provides content moderation APIs, dashboard tools, review queues, analytics, and connector workflows for customer applications.

## Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:

- Having rights to submit content to the service.
- Not submitting illegal content except as necessary for lawful moderation workflows.
- Protecting API keys and dashboard credentials.
- Reviewing moderation decisions before taking high-impact actions where required by law or platform policy.
- Configuring policies appropriate for their audience, region, and product.

## Acceptable Use

Customers may not use Guard API to:

- Harass, surveil, or discriminate against protected groups.
- Build unlawful profiling or targeting systems.
- Submit malware, credential dumps, or intentionally harmful payloads.
- Evade rate limits, billing controls, or abuse protections.

## Data

Guard API may process submitted content, metadata, decisions, audit events, and account data to provide the service. Data retention and deletion rules should be described in the Privacy Policy and customer agreement.

## Billing

Paid plans renew monthly unless cancelled. Usage credit quotas, overages, trials, refunds, and cancellation behavior should match the pricing page and checkout terms.

## Availability

The service is provided on a commercially reasonable basis. Beta and trial features may change, be limited, or be discontinued.

## Liability

Moderation decisions are risk signals, not legal advice. Customers remain responsible for final enforcement decisions in their products.
